About Resume Builder

Resume Builder is an easy-to-use web application designed to help students, fresh graduates, and professionals create beautiful ATS-friendly resumes within minutes. Our goal is to simplify resume creation with modern templates and an intuitive interface.

Our Mission

To provide an accessible platform where anyone can build a professional resume without requiring design skills.

Our Vision

To become a trusted online resume-building platform that empowers job seekers to present their skills effectively.

Why Choose Us?

• ATS-Friendly Templates
• Easy-to-Use Interface
• Instant Resume Preview
• PDF Download Support
• Mobile Responsive Design
